Meet Our Cultural Teams!

Octavium

Octavium, the official band of KJ Somaiya College of Engineering, Mumbai, is a musical band where harmonies merge and rhythms resonate! Thriving in competitions, Octavium values dedication over accolades, gaining exposure and networking opportunities.

Palinoia

Palinoia represents the Indian classical dance wing of the cultural fest. With mind-blowing expressions, and stellar performances, this team brings India cultural and the ethinicity of classical dance form.

Rhapsody

Rhapsody Is the Official Drama team of Kjsce, their plays and drama make every one toughtful and exciting about various topics in society.

Gyrations

Gyrations is the dance squad that electrifies the stage with powerful moves. From hip-hop to classical dance forms, Gyrations captivates the audience with their breathtaking choreography.

Yugandhar

Yugandhar is the brain behind the intellectual side of the fest, organizing debates, quizzes, and brain-teasing challenges. They foster creativity through knowledge and critical thinking.

Insignia

Insignia represents the visual and fine arts team. Whether it's painting, sketching, or sculpture, this team turns imagination into masterpieces, spreading art across the campus.
